Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer urgent/emergency services?

Yes, we offer urgent services for most phone and computer repairs. Emergency services are available for critical situations like data recovery before important deadlines. Contact us directly for emergency service availability and pricing.

What are your operating hours?

We are available Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sundays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Evening and weekend appointments are available to accommodate your schedule. Contact us via WhatsApp or phone to schedule an appointment.

Do you offer remote assistance?

Yes, we provide remote assistance for software issues, virus removal, computer optimization, and assignment consultations. For hardware repairs and physical services like printing, in-person service is required. Remote services are available countrywide.

How long does FRP bypass take?

Most FRP bypass procedures take between 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on the phone model, Android version, and specific issue. Some newer models may require more time. We provide time estimates before starting any work.

Do you provide service across Zambia?

We offer countrywide online support, remote assistance, and assignment services. For in-person repairs, we primarily serve the Lusaka area but can arrange special appointments in other major cities. Shipping arrangements can be made for devices.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money (Airtel Money, MTN Money, Zamtel Money), and bank transfers. For assignment services, we require a 50% deposit with the balance upon completion. All prices are in Zambian Kwacha.
